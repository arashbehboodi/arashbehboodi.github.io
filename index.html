Arash Behboodi

I am a research scientist and Director of Engineering at Qualcomm AI Research. I am interested in general in theoretical foundations of information and computational systems. My research interests include among others information theory, learning theory, machine learning for inverse problems, compressed sensing, geometric deep learning, differentiable simulation and agentic AI.
