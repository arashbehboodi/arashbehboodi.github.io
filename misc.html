Events

  • CoSIP retreat on compressed sensing and learning theory was successfully held in Spa, Belgium on September 12-13. The retreat was composed of gradate students and senior researchers from the group of prof. Rodulf Mathar (RWTH-TI), prof. Holger Rauhut (RWTH-LS C) and prof. Massimo Fornasier (TUM).

  • Compressed Sensing in Information Processing (CoSIP) is a priority program (SPP) established by the DFG. First CoSIP colloquium took place in Aachen, Germany, on July 4 and 5, 2016.
