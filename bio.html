I am Arash Behboodi, Senior Researcher at Institute for Theoretical Information Technology in RWTH Aachen University. My research interests include theoretical aspects of complex networks and data science, information theory and compressive sensing.

Education

  • B.Sc. in electrical engineering (communication systems), Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, 2005

  • M.Sc. in electrical engineering (communication systems), Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, 2007

  • Master in Philosophy, Universite de Paris 1- Pantheon Sorbonne, Paris, France, 2011

  • PhD in telecommunication, Ecole superieure d'electricite (Supelec), 2012

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning

  • Compressed Sensing

  • Information Theory

  • Network Information Theory

  • Stochastic Geometry

  • Localization, Heterogeneous Networks, Quality-aware Communication
